Jasprit Bumrah

Jasprit Jasbirsingh Bumrah (born 6 December 1993) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the Indian cricket team in all formats of the game. A right-arm fast bowler with a unique bowling action, Bumrah is currently considered one of the best limited overs bowlers in the world.[4] He plays for Gujarat and Mumbai Indians in domestic cricket. He is the vice-captain of the Indian team in Test cricket since December 2023.
