KL Rahul

Kannanur Lokesh Rahul (/kənuːr loʊkeɪʃ ˌrɑːhʊl/; born 18 April 1992) is an Indian international cricketer. A right-handed wicketkeeper-batsman, Rahul plays for Karnataka at the domestic level and captains the Lucknow Super Giants in the Indian Premier League.

Personal information

  • Born: 18 April 1992 (age 31years) Bangalore, Karnataka, India
  • Height: 180 cm,, 5'11"(5 ft 11 in)

Domestic Team Information

  1. Karnataka: 2010–present
  2. Royal Challengers Bangalore: 2013, 2016
  3. Sunrisers Hyderabad: 2014–2015
  4. Punjab Kings: 2018–2021
  5. Lucknow Super Giants: 2022–present

International Information

  • Test debut (cap 284): 26 December 2014 v Australia
  • T20I debut (cap 63): 18 June 2016 v Zimbabwe
  • ODI debut (cap 213): 11 June 2016 v Zimbabwe.
