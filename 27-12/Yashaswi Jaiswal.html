Yashasvi Jaiswal

Yashasvi Bhupendra Kumar Jaiswal (born 28 December 2001) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the Indian cricket team. He made his international debut in the first Test against the West Indies in July 2023, scoring a century in his first innings in Test cricket.[3] Domestically he plays for Mumbai and IPL franchise Rajasthan Royals. In 2019, he became the youngest cricketer to score a List A cricket double century,[4] and in the 2020 Under-19 Cricket World Cup, he was the leading run-scorer for India under-19s.
